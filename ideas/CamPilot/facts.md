## The Pain: CAM Reconciliation for Small Commercial Property Managers

### Source 1: BiggerPockets Forum (March 14, 2026 — 4 days ago)
**URL:** https://www.biggerpockets.com/forums/32/topics/1281484-best-pm-software-for-a-small-commercial-portfolio-under-20-units-nobody-has-answe

Direct quote from active property manager in Calabasas, CA managing <20 NNN units:
> "I've been on a spreadsheet + QuickBooks combo and it works until CAM reconciliation season, at which point it turns into a nightmare."

Identified the exact gap:
- Yardi/MRI = "massive overkill for my size, priced for institutional operators"
- AppFolio = "primarily residential... CAM tracking is manual, lease structure flexibility is limited"
- Buildium/DoorLoop/Rent Manager = "built for units-based residential PM... not designed for NNN reimbursements, escalation clauses, or year-end reconciliation"
- RealPage = "Enterprise only, pricing not publicly available, minimum portfolio size requirements"
