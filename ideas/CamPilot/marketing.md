# CamPilot — Marketing & Distribution

## Core Message
"Stop spending January in Excel. CamPilot reads your leases and does your CAM reconciliations in hours, not weeks."

## Target Customer
Small commercial property managers:
- 5-30 NNN leases (retail, office, industrial, mixed-use)
- Currently using QuickBooks + Excel
- Too small for Yardi/MRI, frustrated with AppFolio/Buildium limitations
- Typically: independent owner-operators, regional family offices, small CRE firms

## Channel 1: BiggerPockets (Highest priority)
- BiggerPockets forums are where this persona lives — proven by our research
- Create free educational content: "CAM Reconciliation Guide for Small CRE Operators"
- Post genuine help threads in r/CommercialRealEstate and BiggerPockets forums
- Run BiggerPockets Pro ads targeting "commercial real estate" members
- Offer free first reconciliation to forum members
- Cost: $0-500/mo

## Channel 2: CPA/Accounting Firm Partnerships
- Small CRE accountants who currently do CAM manually for clients are ideal referral partners
- Offer revenue share: 20% commission for referrals
- Target firms with CRE specialization listed on AICPA directory
- Pitch: "Your clients send you spreadsheets every year. Give them CamPilot instead."
- Tool of choice for accountants = tool of choice for their clients

## Channel 3: LinkedIn Content — "CAM Reconciliation Hell" Series
- Weekly posts targeting commercial property managers
- Hook: "Every January, property managers lose $25,000+ in uncollected CAM charges. Here's why."
- Share anonymized examples of common CAM mistakes and how to catch them
- Run targeted LinkedIn ads to title: "property manager" AND "commercial real estate"

## Channel 4: IREM / BOMA Conference Circuit
- Institute of Real Estate Management (IREM) and BOMA International are the two key professional associations
- Sponsor workshop: "AI-Powered CAM Reconciliation: Catch What You're Missing"
- Speaking slot at regional chapter meetings = immediate credibility
- IREM members pay $100K/yr for Yardi; they'll pay $300/mo for a tool that works

## Channel 5: Direct Outreach to Small CRE Firms
- Pull commercial PM firms from Loopnet, CoStar, state DLRE license databases
- Filter for small firms (1-10 employees) with commercial specialization
- Cold email sequence: "You probably lose $15K/year in uncollected CAM. Want to find out?"
- Offer: Free audit of their current year reconciliation to show the gap

## Channel 6: YouTube / Content SEO
- Series: "How to Do CAM Reconciliation for [Retail/Office/Industrial] Properties"
- Target keywords: "CAM reconciliation software," "NNN lease reconciliation template," "how to calculate CAM charges"
- These searches have high intent from small operators who are googling for help right now

## Launch Strategy
1. **Beta**: Reach out directly to 20 small CRE firms through BiggerPockets/LinkedIn. Offer free first year for feedback.
2. **Case Study**: One good case study showing $30K recovered in missed CAM charges = marketing goldmine.
3. **ProductHunt**: Launch with "Stop doing CAM reconciliations in Excel" hook
4. **Partner with one regional IREM chapter** for credibility

## Retention
- The sticky part: once a property manager uploads all their leases, switching cost is very high (reprocess 20 PDFs elsewhere). Network effect from multi-year history.
- Annual reconciliation cycle = natural renewal moment every January
- Add ongoing value: mid-year CAM estimate updates, tenant billing automation
