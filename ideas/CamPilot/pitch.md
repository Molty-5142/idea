# CamPilot — Investor One-Pager

## The Problem

Every January, 60,000+ small commercial property managers enter spreadsheet hell.

They manage 5-30 NNN leases on retail centers, office buildings, and industrial properties. Each lease has custom CAM (Common Area Maintenance) rules — different caps, exclusions, gross-up provisions, and base year stops. They have to reconcile these against actual operating expenses for each tenant.

**The result:**
- 400+ hours per manager per year spent on manual CAM reconciliation (Leasey.AI, 2025)
- 5-15% of recoverable expenses lost to calculation errors
- Tenant disputes from unclear calculations create legal exposure
- Zero affordable software options: Yardi/MRI cost $50-100K+/yr, AppFolio/Buildium can't handle NNN complexity

This is a $26.55B software market (Fortune Business Insights, 2025) with a 60,000+ operator segment completely underserved.

**Forum evidence (BiggerPockets, March 2026):**
> "I've been on a spreadsheet + QuickBooks combo and it works until CAM reconciliation season, at which point it turns into a nightmare."

## The Solution: CamPilot

CamPilot is an AI-native CAM reconciliation platform for small commercial property managers.

**How it works:**
1. Upload lease PDFs → AI extracts CAM terms (inclusions, exclusions, caps, gross-ups, base years)
2. Connect QuickBooks / upload expense data → AI applies per-tenant lease rules
3. Generate auditable reconciliation statements for each tenant
4. Get a recoveries report showing missed charges

**The AI advantage is genuine**: CAM clause language varies per-lease, per-attorney. Only LLM reasoning can read "Tenant's share excludes capital expenditures unless amortized over useful life not to exceed 15 years at a 5% carrying cost" and apply it to actual expense data. Spreadsheets can't. Residential PM tools can't. This requires AI.
