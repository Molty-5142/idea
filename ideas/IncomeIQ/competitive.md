# IncomeIQ — Competitive Analysis

---

## Positioning

IncomeIQ is a **pre-documentation income pre-screening tool** for loan officers working with self-employed borrowers. It lives at the TOP of the origination funnel — before documents are collected.

No current competitor occupies this exact position.

---

## Direct Competitors

### Rapidio (rapidio.com)
- **What it does**: Uploads tax returns/bank statements → produces underwriter-ready income worksheet in <20 minutes
- **Stage in funnel**: POST-document collection
- **Pricing**: Per-file model (~$15-35/file estimated; pricing not fully public)
- **Target**: Brokers, processors, underwriters, small-to-mid lenders
- **Strength**: Human-reviewed accuracy, GSE-approved, NAMB/AIME partner
- **Weakness**: Requires docs. Can't help LO decide WHETHER to collect docs.
- **Gap**: No doc-free pre-screen. No "should I even proceed?" decision support.

### Scenario AI by Zeitro (zeitro.us)
- **What it does**: AI chatbot for guideline lookup — "does this scenario qualify under Fannie Mae guidelines?"
- **Launched**: January 7, 2026
- **Pricing**: Free tier + cheap paid upgrade
- **Strength**: Guideline accuracy, source-backed answers, non-QM coverage
- **Weakness**: Guideline lookup ≠ income calculation. Tells you IF it qualifies, not HOW MUCH.
- **Gap**: Doesn't calculate qualifying income, doesn't handle "tell me based on verbal description"

### Ocrolus (ocrolus.com/mortgage)
- **What it does**: Enterprise document OCR + income calculation + condition management
- **Integrations**: Encompass (Ellie Mae), all major LOS
- **Target**: Large lenders, banks, mortgage companies with >100 loans/month
- **Pricing**: Enterprise contracts, $50K+/year
- **Weakness**: Not for independent brokers. Way too expensive.
- **Gap**: No pre-screening; built for post-submission processing

### Fannie Mae / Freddie Mac Tools (Free)
- Fannie Mae: Income Calculator (free downloadable Excel)
- Freddie Mac: LPA (Loan Product Advisor) income analysis
- **Weakness**: Requires all documents. Complex Excel worksheet. Not conversational. Doesn't cover non-QM.

### Essent Guaranty Income Calculators (Free)
- Free Excel calculators for QM income analysis
- Same weakness: doc-dependent, not conversational

## Indirect Competition

| Tool | Problem | Why Not a Threat |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| Mortgage Maker | LO presentation / rate comparison | Different stage (post-qualification) |
| Homebot | Client retention / equity digests | Not income analysis |
| LoanSifter | Product pricing engine | Not income analysis |
| Generic AI (ChatGPT, Claude) | Unstructured guideline chat | Not mortgage-specific, LO won't trust |

---

## Competitive Moat

1. **Training data**: As IncomeIQ processes thousands of scenarios, it can tune prompts and edge cases to specific guidelines that generic AI misses
2. **Guideline freshness**: Fannie/Freddie/FHA/VA/non-QM guidelines update quarterly — maintaining this is ongoing work that creates barrier to entry
3. **Trust / accuracy**: First mover in doc-free pre-screening, if accuracy is high, becomes the default habit

---

## Critical Watchpoints

- **Zeitro Scenario AI expansion**: They're closest in category. If they add an income estimation mode, they're a direct competitor. Monitor monthly.
- **Rapidio adding pre-screen mode**: Lower risk (they're doc-processing focused), but watch for product roadmap changes.
- **LOS vendors (Encompass, Byte)**: Large LOS players could add AI intake wizards. Low near-term risk (slow-moving enterprise software).
