# IncomeIQ — Marketing & Distribution

## Primary Channel: The Broker Community

### 1. Reddit r/loanoriginators (35K members, highly active)
- Post genuine case studies: "I stopped wasting 90 min on self-employed borrowers. Here's what changed."
- Don't pitch. Show the workflow. Let LOs self-identify with the pain.
- Comment threads where people complain about SE income nightmares — be helpful, link resource

### 2. NAMB (National Association of Mortgage Brokers) — 18,000 member brokers
- Just banned trigger leads — brokers are hungry for tools that help them maximize conversion of every inbound lead
- NAMB has a vendor marketplace. Apply as a preferred vendor.
- Conference presence: NAMB National (fall) is prime

### 3. AIME (Association of Independent Mortgage Experts)
- 14,000 independent broker members
- They have partner programs (Rapidio uses this channel)
- Launch a co-branded resource: "The Self-Employed Income Pre-Screen Checklist"

### 4. Mortgage Facebook Groups
- "Mortgage Pros" — 50K+ members
- "Non-QM Mortgage Professionals" — 8K members
- Post real scenarios, invite LOs to test the tool free

## Partnership Distribution

### Wholesale Lenders (Non-QM AEs)
- Every non-QM wholesale lender has Account Executives who train brokers
- Pitch IncomeIQ as a "free tool for your brokers" — AEs would love to give brokers a tool that helps them identify non-QM candidates faster
- Target: Angel Oak, NewFi Wholesale, FNBA, Deephaven

### Mortgage CRM Integrations
- Integrate with Jungo, Shape, BNTouch CRMs
- When an LO marks a borrower as "self-employed," IncomeIQ wizard auto-launches
- CRM integration = distribution without marketing spend

### Mortgage Coach / Mortgage Maker
- Both are LO presentation tools — IncomeIQ becomes the intake wizard before the presentation
