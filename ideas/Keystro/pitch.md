# Keystro — Investor One-Pager

## The Problem

Real estate agents live on their phones — texting leads, following up with buyers, DMing on Instagram, emailing lenders, Slacking their team. Every message matters. The tone, the timing, the words.

The hard part isn't volume. It's *craft under pressure*. A cold lead who just viewed a listing needs a different message than a warm referral. A text to a 28-year-old first-time buyer reads differently than an email to a corporate relocation executive. Getting that right — fast, on your phone, in any app — is the difference between a $15K commission and silence.

Today, agents either:
- Use static TextExpander snippets (dumb, no context — and TextExpander AI is desktop-only)
- Type from scratch every time (slow, inconsistent)
- Copy-paste from ChatGPT (5 steps to do what should take 5 seconds)

There is no AI that sits at the mobile keyboard layer and helps agents say the right thing, in real-time, in any app.

## The Solution

**Keystro** is the AI keyboard built for real estate professionals.

It works from what you're already typing — no screen reading, no cross-app access. As you type, Keystro completes your sentences and suggests contextually appropriate replies based on:
- What you're typing right now
- Your saved persona (agent in Phoenix, buyer specialist, typical client types)
- Real estate domain knowledge baked in

Start typing "following up on the showing at" → Keystro suggests 3 completions that sound like you.

It replaces your mobile keyboard with one that:
- **Completes your sentences** as you type, in any app
- **Suggests tone variants** — more professional, add urgency, keep it casual
- **Knows real estate language** — listings, leads, escrow, commission, showing requests
- **Learns your voice** via persona config so suggestions sound like you, not ChatGPT
- **Works everywhere** — iMessage, Gmail, Slack, Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, any app

One keyboard. Every app. Always the right message.

## Why Now

1. **LLMs are fast enough for real-time keyboard use** — 2026 models generate a quality reply in < 300ms
2. **Context-from-typing is proven** — Compose AI (desktop) shows this UX works; nobody has done it as a mobile keyboard
3. **Real estate agents are AI-ready** — NAR survey shows 57% actively experimenting with AI tools; they know what AI writing assistance is
4. **TextExpander AI (2025) serves desktop** — mobile is still the gap, and that's where agents close deals
5. **No one has gone mobile + vertical.** Keyboard AI is horizontal (Gboard, Grammarly) or voice (Willow). Mobile + professional vertical = untouched.

## Why AI — Not Just Templates

TextExpander AI suggests snippets. Keystro generates replies.

Snippet suggestion = "here's a saved phrase that might fit." Reply generation = "here's what to say next based on what you've typed." These are fundamentally different products. The agent doesn't pick from a library — Keystro drafts from context.

The AI advantage compounds: the longer an agent uses Keystro, the more persona-specific the suggestions become. That's switching cost.
