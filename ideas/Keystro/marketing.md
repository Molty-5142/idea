# Keystro — Marketing & Distribution

## Persona B: Real Estate Agents

### Channel 1: Real Estate Facebook Groups & Reddit
- r/RealEstateAgents, r/realestate have hundreds of thousands of members
- Post a video: "Watch me respond to 3 leads in 30 seconds with AI" — no pitch, just demo
- The before/after of switching apps vs. typing in one keyboard is visually compelling

### Channel 2: Real Estate Influencers / Coaches
- Real estate has a massive coaching culture (Tom Ferry, Mike Ferry, Buffini, Kevin Ward)
- A single mention from a mid-tier RE YouTuber (50K–500K subs) can generate thousands of sign-ups
- Offer affiliate commissions: $10–$20/referral, these coaches are motivated by economics

### Channel 3: Brokerage Partnerships
- Target independent brokerages with 20–200 agents
- Pitch the team lead / broker-owner: "Give your agents this, they respond to leads 2x faster"
- Team plan pricing ($15–25/agent/mo) + broker dashboard = stickier, higher ACV

### Channel 4: Real Estate Conferences
- Inman Connect, NAR Annual, T3 Summit — agents go to learn tech that makes them money
- A live demo at a conference booth showing 3 lead responses in 60 seconds wins by itself
- Conference circuit is how Follow Up Boss, Dotloop, and Chime got their first enterprise deals

### Channel 5: Product Hunt + Tech Press
- Launch on Product Hunt targeting tech-savvy agents
- Pitch Inman (real estate tech publication) for a feature story — they love AI tools
- "The first AI keyboard built for real estate agents" is a clear hook

### Channel 6: Zapier / Integration Marketplace
- Build integrations with Follow Up Boss, Wise Agent, Sierra Interactive
- These CRMs have partner programs and feature third-party tools to their user bases

## Persona A: Teens & College Students (if pursued)

### Channel 1: TikTok Native Content
- The demo IS the content: "POV you use AI keyboard to text your crush"
- Don't make ads. Make content that looks like a text thread going right
- Pair with trending audio, trending situations (first date texts, group chat drama)

### Channel 2: Discord Community Seeding
- Drop Keystro into active Discord servers (gaming, college, dating)
- Give power users early access + custom vibes to share
- Discord servers have tight word-of-mouth loops

### Channel 3: Viral Mechanics Built In
- "Reply generated by Keystro ✨" subtle watermark on shared screenshots
- Shareable "vibe cards" — export your top replies of the week
- Referral unlock: invite 3 friends to unlock "Unhinged Mode" or premium slang pack

### Channel 4: App Store ASO
- Keywords: "AI text suggestions", "rizz keyboard", "emoji suggester", "what to say app"
- Screenshots showing actual text convos, not generic UI
- Competitor keyword targeting (Rizz!, CharmKey)

### Channel 5: College Campus Ambassadors
- Pay 1–2 students per campus $100–$200/mo to demo and refer
- Works especially well at large state schools with Greek life (high social texting volume)
- Campus ambassador programs scale cheaply before you have a sales team
