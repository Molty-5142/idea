# PolicyPulse — Investor One-Pager

---

## Problem

Small businesses (5–50 employees) are flying blind on employment law. Every year, dozens of state laws change — minimum wage thresholds, overtime exemptions, paid leave rules, classification requirements. Business owners don't track this. They find out when an employee sues them.

**Real quote, January 2026:** *"Compliance has started to feel like a background anxiety that never really goes away... half the time I don't even know if it applies to me or not."* — r/smallbusiness

The average wage-and-hour violation lawsuit costs $40,000+ in settlements and legal fees — and this is what most SMBs are unknowingly walking into.

---

## Solution

**PolicyPulse** is an AI-native employment compliance monitor for small businesses.

- Answer 5 questions (state, headcount, industry, pay types, contractor use)
- PolicyPulse watches every relevant state and federal employment law update
- Monthly digest: "3 laws changed that affect your business this month"
- One-click policy drafting: "Here's your updated overtime policy — ready to use"
- Proactive deadline alerts before compliance windows close

Not a document library. Not a chatbot. A proactive compliance intelligence system.

---

## Why AI Is Essential (Not Optional)

Employment law compliance requires:
- Tracking 50+ state legislatures simultaneously
- Matching changes to specific business profiles (state + size + industry + pay type)
- Drafting updated policy language (not just flagging issues)

A checklist can't do this. A template goes stale. Only AI can continuously parse law changes, apply them to each business's specific context, and generate output in plain English.
