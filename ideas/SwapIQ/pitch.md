# SwapIQ — Investor One-Pager

## TL;DR
**SwapIQ** is the AI-powered replacement property screener for 1031 exchange investors. Upload your property details → get ranked, underwritten replacement candidates → close before the 45-day clock kills your tax deferral.

---

## The Problem

100,000+ 1031 exchanges happen every year in the US. The average investor has **45 days** to find and identify a replacement property or face a capital gains tax bill averaging $50K–$500K.

The current process:
- Manual search across LoopNet, Crexi, Zillow (none of which understand 1031 rules)
- Manual underwriting in spreadsheets per property
- No like-kind compliance check
- No boot/equity requirement calculator
- No countdown or urgency framework

**"Trying to select a property in 45 days is just craziness."** — r/CommercialRealEstate, Feb 2026

The result: investors rush, make bad decisions, overpay, or miss the deadline entirely. IPX1031 (the largest QI) reports failed exchanges are one of their clients' most common regrets.

---

## The Solution

**SwapIQ** is a deadline-aware, AI-native property screener built for the 1031 workflow.

**Step 1: Enter your exchange**
- Relinquished property details, net proceeds, adjusted basis, boot tolerance
- Clock starts automatically (45-day / 180-day countdown)

**Step 2: AI surfaces replacement candidates**
- Integrates with Crexi, LoopNet, or broker-provided listings
- Filters for like-kind compliance automatically
- Upload OM / rent roll PDFs → AI extracts and underwrites

**Step 3: Ranked analysis**
- Cap rate, NOI, debt coverage, boot impact, location risk, lease expiry flags
- "Identify" button generates IRS-compliant property identification letter
- Share with your QI and CPA with one click
